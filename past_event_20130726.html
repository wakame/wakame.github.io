Wakame-vdc introduction and demo

During this meeting we will provide a VirtualBox image with Centos6.4 and Wakame-vdc installed. Using that image we will demo Wakame-vdc and give a workshop on how to use it. It is encouraged to bring your own laptop with VirtualBox installed so you can use the image and follow along with the workshop but this is not required.
